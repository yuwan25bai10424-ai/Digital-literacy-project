## 👋 About This Repository

This repository is my submission for the CSE0001 Digital Literacy project. As part of the **Student Digital Ambassador** scenario, I've worked through five tasks that cover everything from building a professional online presence to understanding cybercrime and how to stay safe online.

I came into this project not really thinking about *why* digital literacy matters I figured if I could use Google and install apps, I was "digitally literate." By the end of it, I realised how much I didn't know: about how UPI scams actually work, about what recruiters see when they look at your GitHub, about what a professional email actually sounds like. This portfolio reflects all of that learning.

## 📌 A Note on What I Learned

If I had to pick one thing from this entire project, it'd be the UPI fraud case study. I grew up watching adults use Google Pay and PhonePe I thought I understood it. But I didn't know that a *collect request* looks almost identical to a payment notification on some apps. The idea that someone could send you a request and trick you into "accepting payment" by entering your PIN and you're actually sending them money that genuinely surprised me. I've already shared the prevention checklist with my family.
